Incremental Model

Incremental Model: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
+ Generates working software quickly and early during the software life cycle. − Needs good planning and design.
+ It is easier to test and debug during a smaller iteration. − Needs a clear and complete definition of the whole system before it can be broken down and built incrementally.
+ Lowers initial delivery cost. − Total cost is higher than with waterfall model.
