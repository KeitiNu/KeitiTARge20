Scrum Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
+ Scrum can help teams complete project deliverables quickly and efficiently − Adopting the Scrum framework in large teams is challenging
+ Works well for fast-moving development projects − If any team member leaves in the middle of a project, it can have a huge negative impact on the project
+ Large projects are divided into easily manageable sprints − Quality is hard to implement until the team goes through an aggressive testing process
